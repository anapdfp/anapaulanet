TEIA

TEIA - Territorios, Educacao Integral e CidadaniA

Description

Responsive website built with Wordpress. Layout developed using Bootstrap.

About

TEIA is a research group created in 2008 at the School of Education (FAE) of Minas Gerais Federal University (UFMG) in Brazil. The group is a network connecting various research activities, teaching and extension in the field of comprehensive education and citizenship. It is thought out as a space for reflection and intervention aimed at ensuring education as an individual and collective right, needed for the purpose of a democratic and inclusive society.

    Logos

    Pacto-MG

    Logo of Pacto, a government program to promote educational process that values and involves teachers of High School in Minas Gerais State, as well as managers and teaching staff of the State Department of Education of Minas Gerais, thus contributing to the improvement of quality of this level of education.

    Webdoc Bagagem

    Logo of Bagagem Webdocumentary. Bagagem in portuguese means "luggage" and the name of the building from which the documentary is about is Maleta, that means "suitcase". The name as well as the logo works as a pun.

    Escola e Cidade

    Logo of the online course "Full-Time Education: the School and the City". The theme of the course is how to use the city as an educational resource, so, the logo displays links connected to form buildings, in a reference of city.

    Skoba.net

    Logo of Skoba.net which means hook, staple, cramp in many slavics languages. The idea of Skoba.Net is providing technical support to IT issues.

    JUVIVA

    Logo of JUVIVA Update Course in which I used the colors of the original project "Juventude Viva" by Brazilian federal government and joined them together using a symbol that represents unity and at the same time give the idea of a link.

    JUBEMI

    Logo of JUBEMI Update Course using the course layout color (orange) and the idea of the mosaic (two mosaic pieces), which permeates the course's theme and methodology.
